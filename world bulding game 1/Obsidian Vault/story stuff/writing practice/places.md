start
	As Ray heads out of town towards lexron there stands an ordinary stone bridge passing over a small river. its no too wide maybe a few meters. the stones look old with a few cracks and chips in the grey rock. its adorned with wood posts at each end on 2 of witch hold signs. one saying path to lexron the other pointing to where Ray came form. As Ray walks along the cold stone, *clack, clack, clack,* each step is noticeable but not bother some. Ray continues for a bit then stops they take a look at the river seeing a few Arns crossing from a distance. ==then== Ray smiles a little. once Ray finishes they leave for their journey.
